The Real Reason

Why Trail Time Powers Our Permits Tab

Every hub we cover has its own permit system: a different website, a WhatsApp bot, a cash-only gate, or a QR code taped to a fence post. Stitching all of that together for riders watching our videos was eating into time we'd rather spend actually riding.

Their Story

Built On Race-Day Timing Technology

Trail Time's roots are in professional timing systems for MTB enduro racing, giving events real-time results with reliable, purpose-built equipment rather than stopwatches and spreadsheets. That same technical backbone, reliable, fast, built for people standing at a trailhead with a phone in their hand, is what makes it well suited to handling day permits too.

That's the same infrastructure now handling online MTB trail permit sales across the region, including the system embedded directly in the Permits tab on wmgoriding.com. For riders, it means a permit that used to require cash, a queue, or a separate app can now be bought in a couple of taps before you even leave the parking lot.
