Disclaimer

Please read the following carefully before using this site or riding any of the trail networks featured.

Liability Disclaimer

Neither Warren & Melissa Go Riding nor anyone associated with Warren & Melissa Go Riding will be responsible or liable for any injury sustained while riding the trail networks we film.

Medical Advice

Consult a doctor before starting any exercise program. This applies to all riders regardless of age or fitness level.

Extreme Sport Warning

Mountain biking is an extreme sport. Riders should be aware of the dangers at all times. Failure to take appropriate precautions may result in serious injury or death.

Ride Smart

Always ride within your limits and in groups where possible. Never ride alone on remote trails. Know your ability and choose trails accordingly.

Working Farms

Many trails featured on this site are situated on working farms. Trail conditions are subject to change without notice or indication. Always check current conditions before visiting a hub.

By using this site and watching our content you acknowledge that you have read and understood this disclaimer. Ride safe, have fun, and look out for each other out there.

Why We Ride

Giving Back
to the Cycling
Community.

Our reason for existing is simple... We want to give back to the cycling community. Mountain biking has given us so much, and this is our way of returning the favour. We want every rider in the Western Cape, and every rider thinking about trying MTB for the first time, to have a free resource they can turn to.

Give Back

For the Community

Mountain biking doesn't need to be complicated or intimidating. Our content is completely free no paywalls, no subscriptions. Just one community sharing the joy of riding with another.

Inspire

Old & New Riders

Whether you've been riding for 20 years or you're thinking about your very first trail, our videos are here to spark something. To show you what's out there. To make you want to get on a bike and go.

Share

The Joy of MTB

The Western Cape has world-class trails on its doorstep. We want as many people as possible to know about them, ride them, and fall in love with mountain biking the same way we did.

The Riders

Warren
& Melissa

Cape Town · Western Cape

We're two riders from Cape Town who genuinely love this sport. We're not professionals, we're not sponsored athletes we're just people who fell in love with mountain biking and wanted to share that with as many others as possible.

Every weekend we can, we're out there riding a new trail, filming it, and bringing it back to the channel. Not because we have to because we genuinely want to.

If even one person watches one of our videos and decides to try mountain biking for the first time, we've done exactly what we set out to do.
