Stoke Suspension Works

Cape Town's suspension specialists, and true all-round bike experts, based in Observatory.

The Real Reason

Why We Trust Stoke With Our Suspension

A fork or shock that's even slightly off changes how a trail feels, and on the rocky, technical lines we film every week that difference shows up as much in the footage as it does in our hands. We don't have time to be guessing about our setup before a shoot day.

Stoke is family. They're always available, always helpful, always kind and accommodating, and most of all, they're incredibly knowledgeable. Suspension is in the name, but that's really just their speciality. They're bike geniuses who never shy away from anything bike related, general maintenance, spare parts and full servicing included.

Stoke has built a reputation in Cape Town for straight, honest service, no upsell, no vague diagnosis, just a clear answer and a proper fix. That's exactly what two riders running a weekly content schedule need from a partner they can trust with more than just suspension.

Their Story

Cape Town's Suspension Specialists

Stoke Suspension Works is a Cape Town shop based in Observatory, built around suspension as their core speciality, forks, shocks and dropper posts, but not limited to it. General bike maintenance, spare parts and full servicing are part of the offering too, which is unusual for a shop this well known for one thing in particular. Riders consistently point to the same things in their feedback: accurate turnaround estimates, fair pricing, and suspension that comes back performing noticeably better than when it went in.

The business marked a fresh chapter recently, reopening under what they're calling "Stoke 4.0", a sign of a shop that's kept evolving rather than standing still. For two riders who put both suspension and the rest of the bike through repeated abuse on rocky Western Cape singletrack, having a genuinely knowledgeable, all-round shop a short drive away is a real advantage.
